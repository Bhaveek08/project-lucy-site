Jul 2026

Her body went motion-capture

The procedural puppet era is over: her wave, dance, bow, goodbye, walking and turns now come from real human mocap — a 5,600+ clip library (Bandai Namco Research + CMU) flowing through a one-command BVH→FBX→animator pipeline. She even fulfilled two of her own old wishes from her request log: she asked to "run and play" months ago, and now she has real running mocap.

Jul 2026

She learns poses from photographs

Hand her any photo and she takes the pose: MediaPipe reads the 3D skeleton, her new pose-lift converts it into muscle space calibrated against her actual rig, and a closed render loop verifies the result numerically. Built after four rounds of hand-tuning proved humans guess muscle values badly.

Jul 2026

Living hair, living clothes — with self-collision

Her model files shipped with zero cloth physics — the hair was rigid all along. Spring chains are now authored automatically onto all 100+ hair/skirt/ribbon bones, with body colliders so nothing clips through her. She moves like she's actually there.

A mind that edits itself + the road on-device

Her meta-agent now grades its own advice: rules evolved from her recurring failures are kept only if her fail rate measurably drops, retired if not — across coding, reasoning, presenting and motion. Proven rules get distilled into her weights. Meanwhile her pocket-sized 1B mind is training overnight, headed for a phone near her.
